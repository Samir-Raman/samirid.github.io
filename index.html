Imagine Dragons

About the Band

Imagine Dragons is a band that plays pop rock, indie rock, electro-pop and alternative rock. The band originated in Las Vegas, Nevada and has become quite popular now.

Members:

  • -Dan Reynolds: Singer (Las Vegas, NV)
  • -Wayne Sermon: Guitarist (American Ford, UT)
  • Danile Platzman: Drummer/Percussion (Atlanta, GA)
  • Ben Mckee: Bass Guitarist (Forestville, CA)
